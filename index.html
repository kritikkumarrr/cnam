Know About Us

At CNAM, we believe that every individual deserves the highest quality of care when it comes to their hearing health. Dr. Rinshul Sharma is a passionate and experienced speech and audiologist dedicated to helping patients of all ages achieve better hearing and communication.

With advanced training and expertise in the latest diagnostic and treatment techniques, Dr. Rinshul Sharma offers comprehensive services tailored to each patient's unique needs. Whether you're experiencing hearing loss, tinnitus, or speech difficulties, we're here to help you find effective solutions and improve your quality of life.

  • Diagnostic Hearing Evaluations.
  • Hearing Aid Fittings.
  • Tinnitus Management.
  • Speech Therapy.
  • Auditory Processing Disorder.
  • Services under one roof.

Why Choose Us ?

Personalized Care: Dr. Rinshul Sharma takes the time to listen to your concerns and develop customized treatment plans tailored to your unique needs and goals. Advanced Technology: We utilize state-of-the-art equipment and the latest techniques to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments. Compassionate Approach: We understand the impact that hearing loss and communication difficulties can have on your life, and we're committed to providing compassionate care and support every step of the way. Convenient Location: Our clinic is conveniently located at Tagore Garden, making it easy for you to access the care you need.

Diagnostic Hearing Evaluations

Our thorough hearing assessments help identify any hearing issues and determine the best course of treatment.

Hearing Aid Fittings

We offer a wide range of hearing aid options to suit your lifestyle and preferences, with personalized fittings for optimal comfort and performance.

Tinnitus Management

If you're bothered by ringing or buzzing in your ears, our tinnitus management programs can provide relief and improve your quality of life.

Speech Therapy

Whether you're struggling with speech articulation, language development, or voice disorders, our speech therapy services can help you communicate more effectively.

Adult speech and language

We are committed to providing education, guidance and support to individuals and caregivers to promote wellness and prevention of new disorders while decreasing the impact of our clients’ communication challenges.

Auditory Processing Disorder (APD) Evaluation and Treatment

We specialize in diagnosing and treating APD to improve auditory processing skills and enhance communication abilities.
